Founding fathers

Fred Beardsley was one of the 'founding fathers' of what we know today as Arsenal Football Club. A keen goalkeeper, Beardsley grew up in Nottingham, playing for Forest and working at a local government munitions factory.

Dial Square

In late 1886, a gaggle of workers from the Woolwich Arsenal Armament Factory decided to form a football team. They called themselves Dial Square as a reference to the sundial atop the entrance to the factory.
